Hi, I’m Hart. This site is a public notebook for clear, practical technical writing: the kind of posts that explain what worked, what broke, and what I would do differently next time.

I’m interested in reliable systems, useful automation, Linux operations, personal infrastructure, and hardware projects that solve real problems. The goal of this blog is to make those experiments easy to revisit and useful to others.

## What you’ll find here

- Step-by-step debugging notes and recovery playbooks
- Writeups on small infrastructure and automation projects
- Project logs for hardware, 3D printing, and tools
- Occasional notes on engineering process and maintainability
